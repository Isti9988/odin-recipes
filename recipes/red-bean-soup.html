Red bean soup

Description

A red bean soup with sour cream

Ingredients

  • 4 l water
  • 0,5 kg red beans
  • 0,5 l sour cream
  • 1 onion per portion
  • 0,5 tbsp salt
  • 0,25 tbsp black pepper
  • 0,25 tbps red pepper
  • 2 bay leafs

Steps

  1. Soaking beans in 2 l hot water for 1 hour
  2. Adding 2 l water and the bay leafs -- cooking them for 45 minutes
  3. Adding sour cream (0,45 l sour cream + 2 tbsp wheat flour + 1 glass of water + 3 tbsp pot liquor) + salt + black pepper + red pepper -- cooking them for 15 minutes
  4. Adding 1 onion per portion
