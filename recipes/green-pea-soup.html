Green pea soup

Description

A green pea soup with carrot and spatzle

Ingredients

  • 4 l water
  • 1 kg green peas
  • 2 carrots
  • 300 g spatzle
  • 4 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 0,5 tbsp salt
  • 0,5 tbsp red pepper
  • 1 g parsley

Steps

  1. Warming up the sunflower oil
  2. Roasting carrots for 2-3 minutes
  3. Adding water, green peas, wheat flour, red pepper, salt and sugar -- cooking them for 30 minutes
  4. Adding spatzle (2 eggs + 250 g whole flour + 0,5 tbsp salt + 0,5 glass of water mixed up) and parsley -- cooking them for 45 minutes
