Green bean soup

Description

A green bean soup with roux

Ingredients

  • 4 l water
  • 1,5 kg green beans
  • 0,5 l roux
  • 0,5 tbsp salt
  • 1 g parsley

Steps

  1. Adding water, green beans and salt -- cooking them for 30 minutes
  2. Adding roux (6 tbsp sunflower oil warmed up + 4 tbsp whole flour -- mixed up + 0,5 tbsp red pepper + 0,45 l sour cream + 2 glasses of water + 3 tbsp pot liquor -- mixed up) and parsley -- cooking them for 15 minutes
